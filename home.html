Traceability, Transparency & Trust

Our client partners experience an increase in sales by at least 30% by introducing Blockchain powered Organic Ledger.


By sharing the 'farm to fork' story of our products, we aim to build trust and engagement among our consumers. Our commitment to providing traceable products ensures that we can back up your product claims, such as being organic or non-GMO, and maintain the trust of our customers.


Our approach to transparency and sustainability extends to our entire supply chain. We incentivize all participants in our supply chain to provide critical and accurate data, reducing inefficiencies and meeting the transparency and sustainability goals set by our customers. We believe that this approach not only benefits our customers and their trust in our products but also leads to market premiums.

  • End to End traceability assuring Food safety and Quality.
  • Shared collaborative platform building mutual trust among players.
  • Tamper proof records to prove authenticity of products
  • Adherence to Quality standards for Regulatory Compliance
  • Trustworthy Consumer brands with a QR code product story

Organic Ledger is a complete solution for all your needs

Organic Ledger is a full stack blockchain powered Agritech solution that provides transparency, traceability, and trust in the agricultural supply chain. We now have an agri ERP system, blockchain-powered marketplace, satellite mapping, advisory services, data analytics, and customer connection. Our farmer’s App Kisan Ledger aims to maximize per-acre value, promote regenerative agricultural practices, and reduce the agri carbon footprint using cutting edge technology to assist farmers with better production and improved earnings.
